My Story

I'm a passionate advocate for resilience, mindset, and functional health—an advocacy grounded in my personal story of transformation. When diagnosed with Parkinson's Disease in 2017 at age 51, I faced debilitating symptoms including muscle rigidity, tremors, and severe sleep disturbances.

Refusing to accept a progressive decline, I took an unconventional approach. I combined high-intensity physical and cognitive exercises to "train my brain" and reclaim control. Through CrossFit, I found a program that paired physical intensity with cognitive challenge, allowing me to regain 95% of my function—an outcome that continues to astonish my neurologists.

By October 2021, I was symptom free. My journey reflects a commitment to thrive despite adversity, using resilience and innovation. As a former British Army officer, I bring mental toughness and discipline to everything I do.

My mission now is to help others discover how they can thrive despite whatever challenges they face, whether at home, at work, on the sports pitch, or during periods of illness.

Services

Platform Speaker

Engaging and inspirational talks for corporate events, conferences, and community gatherings. I share my journey and the principles that can help anyone thrive despite challenges, captivating audiences with actionable advice on mindset and well-being.

Executive & Team Coaching

I work directly with business leaders and teams to cultivate habits that support both physical and mental performance. Drawing parallels between personal transformation and career success, I emphasize mindset, functional fitness, and sustainable wellness as essential strategies to thrive in high-stress environments.

Resilience Workshops

Interactive workshops designed to help participants develop resilience-based techniques that empower them to lead with energy, focus, and renewed commitment to health. Small and large group formats available, as well as online bespoke programmes.
